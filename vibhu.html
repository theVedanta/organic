Advantages of Organic Farming

  1. The soil is preserved
  2. Organic alternatives are cheaper than synthetic products
  3. Lives of animals are saved
  4. Fertilizers and pesticides pollute soil and water so, they are not used in Organic Farming
  5. If pollution is low people are healthier
  6. Chemical products can be mixed into river or lake water from which animals and people -drink this can cause serious illnesses and even death
